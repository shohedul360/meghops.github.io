Services

We provide a comprehensive security solution to protect sensitive data. Our log analyzer identifies and tracks potential threats while our external assets monitoring keeps you informed of vulnerabilities outside of your infrastructure. Our 360 monitoring provides a complete view of your security posture and helps you stay compliant with regulatory requirements. We also specialize in compliance services for companies to achieve and maintain standard compliance and cost optimization services to forecast future costs and better manage budgets, providing peace of mind and reducing risks of data breaches, regulatory fines and reputational damage.


List Of Services

Automated Compliance

MeghOps offers an automated compliance solution for cloud using AI, that helps organizations comply with industry standards like CIS, PCI, and HIPAA. MeghOps ensures your cloud environment is compliant and secure, allowing you to focus on your core business operations.

Log Analyzer

Log Analyzer is a powerful solution that enables organizations to analyze and understand their log data, providing valuable insights into the security and performance of their cloud environment. It detects potential security threats, performance issues, and also detects secrets passwords and tokens. It includes automated reporting and alerting. It is a valuable tool for organizations to proactively manage their cloud environment security and performance.

Cost Optimization

Cost Optimization solution uses AI to forecast and helps organizations to better understand and manage their cloud costs by providing detailed view of all cloud resources and usage, identifying opportunities for cost savings, and providing automated cost optimization recommendations. It enables organizations to achieve significant cost savings while improving the efficiency and performance of their cloud environment.

MeghEye

MeghEye is an advanced security monitoring solution that detects and alerts on potential security threats in your cloud environment. It provides actionable insights and automated remediation capabilities to quickly and efficiently mitigate identified risks. MeghEye gives you a comprehensive view of your security posture and the ability to proactively identify and address vulnerabilities.
